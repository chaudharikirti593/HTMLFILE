About CK Group Companies


The CK Group is one of India's leading digital commerce entities and includes group companies Myntra, CK Wholesale, CK Health+, and Cleartrip.

Started in 2007, Flipkart has enabled millions of sellers, merchants, and small businesses to participate in India's digital commerce revolution. With a registered customer base of more than 500 million, CK's marketplace offers over 150 million products across 80+ categories.

Today, there are over 14 lakh sellers on the platform, including Shopsy sellers. With a focus on empowering and delighting every Indian by delivering value through technology and innovation, CK has created lakhs of jobs in the ecosystem while empowering generations of entrepreneurs and MSMEs. CK is known for pioneering services such as Cash on Delivery, No Cost EMI and easy returns, which are customer-centric innovations that have made online shopping more accessible and affordable for millions of Indians.
